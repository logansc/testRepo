May 25, 2016

Old friends


Our next comic arrives on Friday! You'll want to be here for that, right? (I bet you will.)


Also on Friday: a new round of reader Q and A! If you've got a question you'd like me to dig into, now's the time to ask it... just pop it in an email with "Q and A" in the subject line!


For those interested, my three pin-up books -- Headcanon I and II, plus the Headcanon II Sketchbook -- are now available in a little bundle for a dollar off! I actually made this bundle item so I could start selling it at VanCAF and future conventions (via download codes). But now that it exists, it's available in the regular online ebook store as well. If you like Sam and Fuzzy and you like sexy/goofy pin-up art, have at it!


-Sam Logan

May 23, 2016

VanCAF'd


Thanks once again, friends, for another great VanCAF! It was awesome to see so many of you at the show this year. I had a killer time, and it looks like you all did, too!


But the show's over now, so back we go... back to a big week of Sam and Fuzzy comics! You will not want to miss these ones, team. See you on Wednesday!


-Sam Logan

May 20, 2016

Vancouver Comic Arts Festival


And extra large comic for you today, friends. And yes, also a bit of a cruel cliffhanger. But some pretty major stuff is just around the corner! Just wait 'til you see the comics that are running next week!


VanCAF weekend is upon us! If you're going to be in on near Vancouver this weekend, I highly recommend swinging by the Roundhouse in Yaletown and checking out the show. It's 100% free to attend, and packed to the rafters with awesome comic artists.


If you're there, you can find me in Hall 2 at booth 02/03. I'll have a wide array of Sam and Fuzzy books and shirts in tow, and I'll be doing plenty of drawing. Come by and say hello!
